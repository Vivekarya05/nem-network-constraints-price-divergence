Visual Storyline

The charts are designed to explain how market price behaviour, network constraints, interconnector flow, and regional price divergence connect to spike classifications and decision intelligence.

  • NSW1 recorded spike intervals while VIC1 did not breach the selected $300/MWh threshold.
  • Constraint activity was elevated during NSW1 spike intervals, but marginal value and violation signals did not explain every spike on their own.
  • Broader interconnector congestion-style signals were present during most NSW1 spike intervals.
  • NSW1-VIC1 price divergence was material in concentrated periods, supporting the network-driven market interpretation.

Final Analyst Message

Project 02 demonstrates an end-to-end network intelligence workflow for the NEM. It starts with cleaned dispatch data, adds market and network features, classifies price spikes by likely driver, and translates those classifications into decision recommendations. The visual pack supports slide creation, study review, and dashboard design.
